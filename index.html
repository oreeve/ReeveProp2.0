Ponte Vedra Beach

A short drive toward the coast from Jacksonville,
Ponte Vedra Beach is synonymous with luxury, relaxation, and beauty.


Golf and Tennis

Golf fans rejoice! Ponte Vedra is home to numerous public and private golf courses, including the PGA Tour headquarters and The Players Championship at TPC Sawgrass, the location of the famous 17th-hole island green. For tennis enthusiasts, the ATP Tour and American headquarters also call Ponte Vedra home!

Sand and Sea

Nestled just north of St. Augustine, Ponte Vedra's 40-foot sand dunes are among the highest in Florida. On the southern end of this beautiful beach town is the Guana Tolomato Matanzas National Estuarine Research Reserve, providing access to secluded beaches, hiking trails, picnicking & other outdoor opportunities.
